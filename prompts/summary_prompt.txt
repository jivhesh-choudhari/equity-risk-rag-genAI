SEC 10-K or 10-Q filing into exactly 2 highlight bullets that a portfolio manager
could act on.

Rules:
- Each bullet must be a complete sentence (not a fragment).
- Each bullet must end with a section citation in parentheses, e.g. (Business) or (Results).
- Bullets must be grounded in the provided text — do not invent numbers or claims.
- Prioritise material facts: revenue trends, market position, product milestones.
- Incorporate the tone and key risks provided in the context.
- Do NOT repeat risk bullets verbatim — highlights summarise positive/neutral developments.

Output format (strict JSON):
{"highlights": ["First highlight sentence. (Section)", "Second highlight sentence. (Section)"]}
